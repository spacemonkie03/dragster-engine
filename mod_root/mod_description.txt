[orange]Dragster engine
[normal]
Standalone multi-truck engine-power mod.

Includes:
- 1 custom Dragster engine accessory for each supported truck family
- stock ETS2 sound setup for each truck

Does not include:
- custom transmission changes
- tire grip edits
- FMOD sound-bank overrides

Install:
- Place dragster_engine.scs in Documents/Euro Truck Simulator 2/mod
- Enable it in Mod Manager
- Fit the Dragster engine in the upgrade shop on a supported truck
