# Title

[ETS2] Dragster engine - open-source engine mod for supported stock SCS trucks

# Body

Hello everyone,

I have released `Dragster engine`, an open-source ETS2 engine mod that adds a custom drag-style engine option to supported stock SCS trucks.

What it does:
- adds a `Dragster engine` accessory to supported trucks
- keeps the stock sound setup for each truck family
- focuses on extreme engine output and acceleration

What it does not include:
- no custom FMOD sound banks
- no tire edits
- no transmission edits

Install:
1. Put `dragster_engine.scs` into `Documents\Euro Truck Simulator 2\mod`
2. Enable the mod in Mod Manager
3. Fit `Dragster engine` in the truck upgrade shop
